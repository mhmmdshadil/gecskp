About the College

Government Engineering College Sreekrishnapuram (GEC SKP), established in 1999 in Sreekrishnapuram, Palakkad, Kerala, is a leading institution dedicated to technical education. Affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE, we offer cutting-edge programs in engineering disciplines.

Our vision is to empower students with knowledge, foster innovation, and drive research to address global challenges.
